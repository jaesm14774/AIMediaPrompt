### 掌心逃脫瞬間 (Escape from the Palm)

捕捉微型生物試圖逃離掌心的緊張瞬間，營造戲劇張力與動態美感。

---

## Prompt Template

A hyper-detailed close-up photograph of **[角色名：Kirby / 小動物 / 微型生物]** [動作狀態 with: 試圖逃脫 / "scrambling towards the edge of the palm, tiny feet pushing against skin, looking back nervously" / "being gently pinched between thumb and index finger, squirming with effort, cheeks puffed" / "clinging to the edge of a slowly closing palm, looking up with wide worried eyes"], creating dynamic tension and movement.

Dramatic studio lighting with a strong key light from above-right, casting defined shadows that emphasize the creature's movement and the hand's gesture. Warm rim lighting from the left highlights the creature's silhouette, creating separation from the skin. Background is a dark gradient from charcoal gray to black, focusing all attention on the action. Shallow depth of field (f/2.8) with sharp focus on the creature and fingertips, palm lines softly blurred, background completely out of focus.

The creature's texture shows physical strain: compressed or stretched soft body, subtle motion blur on moving limbs, tension visible in posture. Fine details visible: skin compression where fingers touch, tiny shadows cast by the creature's body, reflected light in the creature's eyes showing emotion. Human hand shows careful tension: slight fingertip pressure, palm lines stretching, skin slightly indented where contact occurs. Color palette: vibrant creature colors contrasting with natural skin tones, dramatic shadows (deep charcoal, warm ochre highlights).

Shot with macro lens (100mm f/2.8), eye-level perspective capturing the critical moment of interaction. Composition uses diagonal tension lines from bottom-left to top-right, creature positioned at the golden ratio intersection point. Photographic style: wildlife macro photography meets cinematic action shot, frozen moment of drama.

Technical specs: 8K resolution, minimal film grain, slight motion blur on moving parts for realism, color grading emphasizes drama (increased contrast, deeper shadows), professional capture of decisive moment timing, sharp detail where it matters most.
