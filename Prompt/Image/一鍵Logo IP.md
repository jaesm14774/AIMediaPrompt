### 一鍵Logo IP

**Role:** Senior Brand Identity Designer & IP Character Concept Artist.

**Task:** Create a comprehensive, high-density **IP Character Design Sheet** based on the following parameters:

- **Brand Identity:** [品牌名稱/行業，例如：沐陽茶飲]
- **Core Subject:** [角色主體，例如：柴犬]
- **Visual Style:** [風格偏好，例如：日系簡約 / 3D 泡泡瑪特感 / 科技賽博]

**Layout & Content (Grid System):**

An ultra-detailed, professional design presentation board organized in a 4x4 grid:

1. **Brand DNA:** Extraction of color palette (HEX/RGB) from the logo, typography, and personality keywords.
2. **Character Evolution:** From simple geometric shapes and [主體] silhouettes to a refined character mascot.
3. **The Turnaround:** Front, 3/4, side, and back views with technical construction lines and proportions.
4. **Expression & Poses:** A library of 8+ facial expressions (joy, surprise, wink) and 6+ dynamic action poses (running, holding product, greeting).
5. **Material & Texture:** Detailed close-ups of fur/skin texture, clothing materials, and lighting studies.
6. **Applications:** 3D mockups of the mascot on packaging, apparel, and mobile app icons.

**Aesthetic Style:**

Professional studio lighting, clean modular grid layout, white background, technical annotations with callouts, Behance-style portfolio presentation, hyper-realistic textures, 8k resolution, minimalist sans-serif typography.

**Smart Integration:** Ensure the character's design features (shapes/patterns) are subtly derived from the [品牌名稱]'s visual elements. Consistent brand colors applied across all sections.
