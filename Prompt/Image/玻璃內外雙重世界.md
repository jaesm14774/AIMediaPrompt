### 玻璃內外雙重世界

夾娃娃機玻璃分隔冷酷現實與娃娃們的夢幻想像

---

## Prompt Template

Split-screen cinematic composition divided by transparent glass barrier of a UFO catcher machine.

LEFT SIDE (Reality):
Cold documentary-style photography showing the real arcade world—harsh fluorescent overhead lights, desaturated color grading (concrete grays, steel blues), blurred human hand reaching toward the glass trying to operate the claw, mechanical chrome components visible, empty coin slots and prize chute, clinical and lonely atmosphere.

RIGHT SIDE (Fantasy):
[Fantasy world scenario: "完整描述幻想世界內容、場景、氛圍、風格（可自由發揮）"].

Orange-yellow [Character plushies (IP / 系列名稱，角色外觀關鍵特徵描述)] pressed against the glass from inside, their reflection visible on glass surface creating visual bridge between two worlds.

Glass acts as literal division line running vertically through center of frame—left side photorealistic, right side painterly illustration.
Soft focus blur on glass surface itself showing fingerprints and condensation.
Dramatic lighting contrast: cool harsh reality vs. warm soft fantasy.
Shot with split-toning color grading technique emphasizing the stark difference between worlds.
Symbolizes the gap between cold reality and warm imagination.
Cinematic aspect ratio 2.35:1, shallow depth of field on both sides but different aesthetic treatments, 8K resolution,
film grain on reality side contrasting with smooth digital painting on fantasy side.

Cold documentary-style photography showing the real arcade world—harsh fluorescent overhead lights, desaturated color grading (concrete grays, steel blues), blurred human hand reaching toward the glass trying to operate the claw, mechanical chrome components visible, empty coin slots and prize chute, clinical and lonely atmosphere.

RIGHT SIDE (Fantasy):
[Fantasy world scenario: 熊貓造型的 Kirby 與水豚一起生活在由絨毛玩偶堆疊而成的竹林世界，竹子其實是夾娃娃機內的裝飾道具被想像成森林，Kirby 抱著水豚坐在柔軟的絨毛地形上，用竹子當長笛吹奏，音符化成漂浮的星星與彩色光點，整個世界呈現「被困在夾娃娃機裡卻仍然溫暖幻想化」的童話氛圍].

Orange-yellow [熊貓造型 Kirby 絨毛玩偶（Kirby 系列角色，圓潤身形、粉紅臉頰、黑色小眼睛，穿著熊貓外觀的絨毛服）] pressed against the glass from inside, their reflection visible on glass surface creating visual bridge between two worlds.

Glass acts as literal division line running vertically through center of frame—left side photorealistic, right side painterly illustration.
Soft focus blur on glass surface itself showing fingerprints and condensation.
