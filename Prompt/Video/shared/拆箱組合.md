### 以貓 + 貓相關物品 為例 (Hailuo 2)

**Camera Instruction:** Single fixed camera, wide-angle lens, one continuous shot, no camera cuts or movement.

**Main Description:** The scene begins in a sunny, empty room. In the center sits a cardboard box with a cute cat face drawn on it with a marker. The box wiggles slightly, lets out a soft meow, and then bursts open! Countless yarn balls, scratching post parts, soft cushions, and cat toys gush out. A giant cat tree grows rapidly from the floor like a real tree, complete with leafy platforms; a scratching post wraps itself with sisal rope and stands in a corner; feather wands dance through the air before landing in a toy basket. Once everything is perfectly in place, a fluffy orange cat walks in with elegant steps, curiously observing its new paradise, and finally curls up comfortably on a soft cushion.

**Core Elements:** Cardboard box with a cat face, large cat tree, scratching posts and pillars, various cat beds and cushions, wall-mounted cat shelves, a window hammock, an automatic water fountain, cat toys (feather wands, jingle balls, toy mice), a rug with a paw-print pattern.

**Motion & Atmosphere:** Full of playful fun and whimsy, various cat supplies assemble automatically in a lively, charming way, ultimately creating a warm, cozy, and safe paradise built perfectly for a cat.

**Keywords:** 16:9, one take, no cuts, dynamic assembly, photorealistic, cat, cute, cat room, pet paradise, heartwarming, no text.

### 龍貓

**Studio Ghibli Totoro Cat Paradise - Single Take Animation**

**Camera Instruction:** Single fixed camera, wide-angle lens, one continuous shot, no camera cuts or movement, capturing the magical transformation in classic Ghibli style.

**Main Description:** The scene opens in a sunlit, empty room with soft, warm lighting reminiscent of a Ghibli film. In the center sits a large cardboard box decorated with Totoro's iconic face - his wide smile, triangular ears, and gentle eyes drawn in charcoal. The box trembles gently, emitting Totoro's signature low rumbling purr. Suddenly, it bursts open in a shower of sparkles and forest magic!

Countless enchanted objects flow out like woodland spirits: yarn balls that glow with soft bioluminescence, scratching post segments that assemble themselves with vines and moss, plush cushions adorned with acorn patterns, and toys that move with forest magic. A magnificent cat tree grows from the floor like an ancient camphor tree, its branches forming cozy platforms covered in soft moss and tiny glowing soot sprites. Scratching posts wrap themselves in natural hemp rope as if guided by forest spirits, while feather wands dance through the air like floating leaves before settling into a woven basket.

As the magical assembly completes, a fluffy orange cat with Totoro-like markings enters with graceful, almost mystical steps. The cat explores this enchanted paradise with wonder, finally curling up on a cushion embroidered with Totoro's silhouette, as dust motes dance in the golden sunlight streaming through the window.

**Core Elements:** Totoro-faced cardboard box, ancient tree-like cat tower with moss-covered platforms, natural scratching posts with hemp rope, cushions with acorn and leaf patterns, wall-mounted shelves resembling tree branches, window hammock woven like a spider's web, a stone fountain with magical water, cat toys (feather wands like floating leaves, jingling acorn bells, felt mice resembling forest creatures), a rug with paw prints and Totoro footprints.

**Motion & Atmosphere:** Filled with Ghibli's signature magical realism and gentle whimsy, cat supplies assemble through forest magic with floating movements and soft sparkles. The transformation creates a harmonious blend of nature and comfort - a mystical cat sanctuary that feels both magical and peacefully domestic, bathed in warm, golden light with soft shadows.

**Keywords:** 16:9, one take, no cuts, magical assembly, Studio Ghibli style, Totoro aesthetic, forest magic, bioluminescent details, warm lighting, cat paradise, enchanted transformation, heartwarming, no text, soot sprites, mystical realism.
