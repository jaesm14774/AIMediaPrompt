### 拆箱組合聖誕版

**Camera Instruction:** Single fixed camera, wide-angle lens, one continuous shot, no camera cuts or movement.

**Main Description:** The scene begins in an empty, dark room with large windows showing falling snow outside. In the center sits nothing but wooden floorboards. Suddenly, a warm fireplace materializes with crackling flames. Countless Christmas decorations burst into existence and assemble themselves magically - a Christmas tree grows tall with ornaments flying onto branches, string lights wrap around the tree in spirals, stockings hang themselves on the mantle, candy canes and gift boxes arrange themselves perfectly, a wreath hangs itself on the door. Once everything is in place, Santa Claus steps out from behind the tree with a jolly laugh, waving at the camera.

**Core Elements:** Christmas tree with ornaments, string lights, stockings on fireplace, wrapped gift boxes with ribbons, candy canes, wreaths, Santa Claus, falling snow outside windows, crackling fireplace.

**Motion & Atmosphere:** Magical and festive, Christmas decorations assemble automatically in a whimsical, joyful way, ultimately creating a warm, enchanting, and cozy Christmas wonderland.
